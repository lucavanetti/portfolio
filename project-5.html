For Experience Summer Camp, I designed the visual identity system to support a strategic rebranding phase, redefining the brand's key elements during a period of transition and relaunch. The project included the development of a new logo and visual language, crafted to reflect the organization’s educational and sporting values in a more contemporary and distinctive way. The identity was officially unveiled during the annual event at the Fondazione Corriere della Sera, creating a cohesive and recognizable experience tailored for sponsors, partners, and stakeholders.
