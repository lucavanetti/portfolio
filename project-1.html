MPR Italy (Medical Products Research) celebrates twenty years and looks toward the future. The rebranding project has redefined the company's visual identity across every dimension: a new logo, a cohesive visual system, and a graphic language capable of giving shape to the values of research, care, and sustainability that have always distinguished the Lombardy-based firm. This comprehensive overhaul was designed to accompany the launch of LIBERA, the new advanced technology for managing respiratory diseases. It is an identity that blends scientific rigor with visual sensitivity, representing a brand dedicated to caring for people every day.
